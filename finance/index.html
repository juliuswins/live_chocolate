This is intended to help beginners or anybody unfamiliar with investing.

Do not be scared by the prospect of investing: You already have a bank account, you use a currency, you pay tax and you may have superannuation if you're Australian.

This does not consider tax circumstances, brokerage fees of buying and selling assets, nor the fees in funds, which work internally.

It is also worth noting that individual companies can inflate or buy back shares, which will also affect your asset value and makes good management paramount for a suitable long term investment in a company.
